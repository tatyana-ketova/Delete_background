Description:

The program removes the white background from an image. The "threshold" is a parameter that determines the sensitivity of the background removal process. It specifies the level of similarity between the background color and the color to be removed. A threshold value of 1 removes only pure white color, while higher values remove a wider range of white shades. Adjusting the threshold allows users to customize the extent of background removal according to their preferences.

Instruction:

1. Click on the "Upload" button. Please ensure that the image you upload is in a common format such as JPEG or PNG

2. Choose the image file you want to process from your computer, then click "Open" or "Upload".

3. Adjusting the Threshold (Optional): If you want to adjust the threshold for background removal, enter a value in the provided field.

4. Click on the "Download" button to save the processed image to your computer.
